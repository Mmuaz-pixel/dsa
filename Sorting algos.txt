Gnome Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stability: Stable
Comparison-Based: Yes

Pancake Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stability: Not necessarily stable
Comparison-Based: Yes

Pigeonhole Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n + range), where "range" is the difference between the maximum and minimum elements
Space Complexity: O(range)
Stability: Stable
Comparison-Based: No

Comb Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n^2) in worst case, but with an optimal shrink factor, it becomes O(n log n)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stability: Not necessarily stable
Comparison-Based: Yes

Quick Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n log n) on average, O(n^2) in worst case
Space Complexity: O(log n)
Stability: Not stable
Comparison-Based: Yes

Merge Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n log n)
Space Complexity: O(n)
Stability: Stable
Comparison-Based: Yes

Bubble Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stability: Stable
Comparison-Based: Yes

Bucket Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n + k), where k is the number of buckets
Space Complexity: O(n + k)
Stability: Stable
Comparison-Based: No

Counting Sort:

Time Complexity: O(n + k), where k is the range of non-negative integers
Space Complexity: O(n + k)
Stability: Stable
Comparison-Based: No
